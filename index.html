About me

Hi, there! My name is Lívia! :)

I’m a journalist graduated from Faculdade Cásper Líbero and I’m currently working as a reporter for the website CARAS Brasil.

I interview both national and international talents, write my opinions on films, cover events, and make occasional appearances on TV!

I've worked as an Editor for English Features at Her Campus Media magazine; as a Producer and Host of the Edição Extra program on TV Gazeta and as an Entertainment Reporter for journalist Gabriel Perline's column on the Portal iG.

Ah, I produced a webserie too!

Previously, I was the host and producer of the Edição Extra program on TV Gazeta, worked as an entertainment reporter for Portal iG, and served as the English Features Editor at Her Campus Media magazine!
